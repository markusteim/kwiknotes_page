Why KwikNotes?

ZERO‑FRICTION CAPTURE

Jot a quick note in the add‑bar or fire off an email—your thoughts appear in seconds.

EMAIL FROM ANYWHERE

Open any mail app and send an email to your personal KwikNotes address (e.g. alex@kwik-notes.com). Specify a project in the subject—or leave it blank to file under General.

STAY ON TRACK

Hit the ★ Favorite button to drop a note straight onto your Daily list. The floating Today panel follows you so top‑priority tasks never leave your sight.

PROJECT POWER

Create, rename, merge, and drag notes between projects with buttery‑smooth drag‑and‑drop.

IDEATION PAD

A roomy legal‑pad editor with numbered and checkbox lists—perfect for brainstorming in the sidebar or in the pop‑out window.

WORKS ANYWHERE

Encrypted local cache means offline access; everything syncs automatically the moment you’re back online.

Getting Started

  1. Install KwikNotes from the Chrome Web Store.
  2. Pin it: click the puzzle‑piece (Extensions) icon, then tap the pin 📌 next to KwikNotes so it’s always on your toolbar.
  3. Create your account: click the KwikNotes icon, select Create Account, and enter your email & password—your unique note‑capture address appears instantly.
  4. Send your first email:
    • Project: Trip Planning – creates (or tags) the “Trip Planning” project.
    • (no subject) – lands in Today.
    • Write your note in the body and hit send. Open KwikNotes to see it already filed.
